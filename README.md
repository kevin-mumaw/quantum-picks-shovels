# Quantum Computing Ecosystem Screener
### Second Layer Capital | Quantitative Research Framework

A flat, high-utility quantitative stock screening tool designed to isolate strategic "picks and shovels" supply chain bottlenecks across the quantum computing industrial complex.

## 🏗️ Core Ecosystem Layers
The screener segments the global quantum infrastructure architecture across critical supply chain dependency vectors:
1. **Hardware Modality**: Core quantum processing units (Trapped Ion, Superconducting, Neutral Atom).
2. **Control Systems & RF**: Microwave control pulse systems, laser delivery networks, and AWG electronics.
3. **Cryogenics & Environment**: Sub-Kelvin thermal management systems, dilution refrigeration, and shielding.

## 🧮 Quantitative Evaluation Strategy
Assets are systematically ranked utilizing weighted priority scores focused on balance sheet safety and institutional scale:
* **R&D Intensity (40%)**: Research and Development expenses relative to top-line Revenue.
* **Cash Runway (30%)**: Liquid reserves scaled against annual operational net burn rate.
* **Revenue Growth (15%)**: Realized Year-over-Year top-line traction.
* **Margin Direction (15%)**: Gross profit optimization trend lines.
